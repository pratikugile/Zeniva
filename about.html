About Zenvia

Delivering purity, trust, and wellness to every corner of Pune since day one.

Our Story

Zenvia was born out of a simple but powerful idea - every person in Pune deserves access to clean, safe, and pure drinking water. We saw that many families, offices, and event organisers were struggling to find a water brand they could truly trust. That is when we decided to step in and make a difference.

We started our journey with a small facility and a big dream. From the very beginning, our focus has been on quality above everything else. We invested in advanced purification technology, set up strict quality checks at every stage, and built a team that shares our passion for purity. Every bottle of Zenvia water goes through a rigorous 7-stage purification process before it reaches your hands.

Today, Zenvia is one of Pune's most trusted packaged drinking water brands. We proudly serve hundreds of offices, events, hotels, and homes across the city. But for us, the journey is far from over. We continue to work hard every single day to raise the bar for water quality in Pune and to earn the trust of every customer who chooses Zenvia.

Our Vision & Mission

Guided by purpose, driven by passion for pure water.

To become the most trusted and preferred packaged drinking water brand in Pune and across Maharashtra. We envision a future where every person has easy access to pure, safe, and affordable drinking water. At Zenvia, we believe that clean water is not a luxury - it is a basic right that everyone deserves.

To deliver the highest quality packaged drinking water to homes, offices, events, and hotels in Pune through advanced purification, strict quality control, and reliable delivery. Our Values

These core values guide everything we do at Zenvia.

Purity

Purity is at the heart of everything we do. Our 7-stage purification process ensures that every single drop of Zenvia water is free from impurities and completely safe to drink. We never compromise on purity, no matter what.

Trust

Trust is earned, not given. We have built our reputation by being honest, transparent, and consistent. When you see the Zenvia label, you can be confident that you are getting the best quality water every single time.

Quality

Quality is not just a word for us - it is our promise. From sourcing water to packaging and delivery, we follow the strictest quality standards at every step. Our ISO-certified facility is a testament to our commitment to excellence.

Service

Our customers are at the centre of everything we do. We believe in providing exceptional service at every touchpoint - from easy ordering to reliable same-day delivery and responsive customer support around the clock.

Why We Exist

India is home to over 1.4 billion people, and access to clean drinking water remains one of the biggest challenges our country faces. In a rapidly growing city like Pune, the demand for safe and reliable drinking water is increasing every day.

Many households and businesses still depend on water sources that may not be properly treated or tested. This puts the health and well-being of families, employees, and guests at risk. We believe that no one should ever have to worry about whether the water they are drinking is safe.

That is exactly why Zenvia exists. We are here to bridge the gap between the need for clean water and the availability of a trustworthy water brand. With our advanced purification technology, strict quality checks, and dependable delivery network, we make sure that pure water is always within your reach - whether it is for your home, your office, or your next big event.
