Why Zenvia Water?

Every bottle of Zenvia is a promise of purity, safety, and quality you can trust.

7-Stage Purification

Our water passes through a rigorous 7-stage purification process including sediment filtration, activated carbon treatment, reverse osmosis, UV sterilisation, ozonation, mineral balancing, and final micron filtration. This ensures that every single drop is free from contaminants and safe to drink.

BIS Standards

Zenvia water meets and exceeds the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) IS 14543 requirements for packaged drinking water. Our ISO 22000 certified facility undergoes regular third-party audits and quality testing to ensure strict compliance with all national safety and hygiene standards.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

We care about the environment as much as we care about water quality. Our bottles are made from recyclable PET plastic, and we are actively working towards reducing our carbon footprint. We encourage our customers to recycle and are committed to sustainable packaging practices for a greener Pune.
