Our Commitment to Purity

At Zenvia, quality is not just a benchmark -- it is the foundation of everything we do. From the moment water enters our state-of-the-art facility to the instant it reaches your hands, every step is monitored, tested, and verified to ensure absolute purity.

We believe that access to safe drinking water is a fundamental right. That is why we invest in advanced purification technology, maintain strict quality control protocols, and hold ourselves to the highest industry certifications. Our in-house laboratory runs over 50 quality checks on every batch before it leaves our facility in Pune.

7-Stage Purification Process

Our advanced multi-stage process removes impurities at every level while retaining essential minerals for optimal health.

Step 1

Source Water Collection

Water sourced from tested underground bore wells and municipal supply.

Step 2

Sediment Filtration

Removes sand, silt, rust, and large suspended particles.

Step 3

Activated Carbon Filtration

Absorbs chlorine, odors, organic compounds, and discoloration.

Step 4

Reverse Osmosis (RO)

Eliminates dissolved salts, heavy metals, and microscopic contaminants.

Step 5

UV Sterilization

Destroys 99.99% of bacteria, viruses, and harmful microorganisms.

Step 6

Mineral Enhancement

Adds back essential minerals for a balanced pH and great taste.

Step 7

Quality Testing & Packaging

Final lab testing before hygienic automated bottling and sealing.

Our Certifications

Zenvia holds all major national and international certifications for food safety and water quality, ensuring complete peace of mind with every sip.

ISO 22000:2018

International standard for Food Safety Management Systems, ensuring hazard control at every stage of the supply chain from production to delivery.

BIS Certified

Bureau of Indian Standards certification confirms our water meets IS 14543 requirements for packaged drinking water quality and safety parameters.

FSSAI Approved

Licensed by Food Safety and Standards Authority of India, ensuring strict compliance with national food safety regulations and labeling standards.

ISI Mark

The ISI certification mark guarantees that our product conforms to Indian Standard specifications for quality, reliability, and safety benchmarks.
