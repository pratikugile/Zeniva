Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our water delivery service.

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver to all major areas in Pune including Koregaon Park, Viman Nagar, Hinjewadi, Kothrud, Baner, Wakad, Hadapsar, Kharadi, Magarpatta, Camp, Shivajinagar, Deccan, Aundh, and many more. If you are unsure whether we deliver to your area, please give us a call or send a WhatsApp message and we will confirm right away.

What is the minimum order quantity?

There is no minimum order quantity for retail purchases. For bulk orders, the minimum is 1 case (typically 12 or 24 bottles depending on the size). We offer competitive pricing on larger quantities, so the more you order, the better the rate. Contact us for a customized bulk pricing quote.

How quickly can you deliver?

We offer same-day delivery for all orders placed before 2:00 PM on working days (Monday to Saturday). Orders placed after 2:00 PM will be delivered the next business day. For urgent requirements or large event orders, please call us directly and we will do our best to accommodate your timeline.

Do you offer monthly subscriptions?

Yes, we offer both weekly and monthly subscription plans for regular water delivery. Subscription customers enjoy priority delivery, discounted rates, and the convenience of automated scheduling. Whether you need water for your home, office, or business, we can create a plan that fits your consumption needs perfectly.

How do I place a bulk order?

You can place a bulk order in three easy ways: call us directly at +91-97671-94735, send us a WhatsApp message for a quick quote, or fill out the contact form on this page with your requirements. Our team will respond within a few hours with pricing details, delivery schedule, and any customization options available for your order.

Need Water Right Now?

Don't wait. Call us or send a WhatsApp message for immediate assistance. We deliver across Pune with same-day service for orders before 2 PM.
